Paris, France


Paris, known as the "City of Light", is one of the most beautiful and romantic cities in the world. It is famed for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture.

  • Eiffel Tower – The iconic symbol of Paris and a must-visit landmark.
  • Louvre Museum – Home to the world-famous painting Mona Lisa.
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  • Seine River Cruise – Experience Paris from the water with breathtaking views.
  • Montmartre – An artistic hilltop neighborhood full of charm and history.
    • Croissants – A buttery, flaky pastry that’s best enjoyed fresh in the morning.
    • Macarons – Colorful and delicate, these are sweet delights found in every patisserie.
    • Baguette – A traditional French bread that’s crisp on the outside and soft inside.
    • Coq au vin – A classic French dish made with chicken and wine.
    • Ratatouille – A delicious mix of vegetables stewed in olive oil.
